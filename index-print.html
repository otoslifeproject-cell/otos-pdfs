How to use these

Send only after a “yes, send more” reply.

  1. Initial outreach happens first. A short, personal email that asks if the recipient is open to more. Nothing is sent unsolicited.
  2. Wait for a positive reply. A clear “yes, send more”, or its equivalent. Anything less than that, do not attach the brief.
  3. Choose the right route. Investor / funder / strategic? Gareth sends the Angel brief. Public personality / champion / lived-experience advocate? Dean sends the Champion brief.
  4. Send the matching short email. Both are written into the master document. Personalise the opening line. Keep the framing intact.
  5. Attach the corresponding PDF. Print each HTML to PDF (Cmd / Ctrl + P → Save as PDF, A4 portrait, default margins). Recipient line on the cover is optional but useful.
  6. Hold the boundaries in the reply. No clinical claims. No partner-name dropping. No champion endorsement claims. Risk notes in the master doc list what must not be said.
